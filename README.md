# Hospital-Analytics-PowerBI
# 🏥 Hospital Operations & Financial Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides an interactive overview of hospital operations and financial performance. It helps analyze appointments, treatments, patient activity, doctor performance, revenue, and payment status through dynamic visualizations and KPIs.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Monitor hospital appointments and patient activity.
- Analyze treatment costs and treatment distribution.
- Track hospital revenue and payment performance.
- Provide actionable insights for operational and financial decision-making.

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Total Appointments
- Total Doctors
- Total Patients
- Total Revenue
- Total Treatments
- Paid Revenue

---

## 📈 Dashboard Visualizations
- Treatment Distribution (Donut Chart)
- Average Treatment Cost by Treatment Type
- Revenue by Payment Method
- Monthly Treatment Trend
- Bills by Payment Status
- Top Doctors by Appointments *(Optional)*

---

## 🎛 Interactive Filters
- Appointment Date
- Doctor ID
- Treatment Type
- Appointment Status

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Microsoft Excel

---

## 📂 Dataset
The dashboard is built using hospital management data containing:
- Appointments
- Patients
- Doctors
- Treatments
- Billing & Payments

---

## 💡 Business Insights
- Identify the most frequently performed treatments.
- Compare average treatment costs across treatment types.
- Monitor payment status (Paid, Pending, Failed).
- Track monthly treatment trends.
- Analyze revenue generated through different payment methods.
